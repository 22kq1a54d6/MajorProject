Hana yori Dango is a Japanese film released on August 19, 1995, directed by Yasuyuki Kusuda. It is based on the manga series,, written and illustrated by Yoko Kamio. It is the first of the many adaptations of the comics for the screen, and it stars Yuki Uchida as the main character of Makino Tsukushi.
